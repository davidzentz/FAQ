Booking & process

Reach out via the contact form or by email to discuss your project. After a brief conversation to understand your needs, a quote and a session agreement are provided. A deposit is required to hold your date, with the remaining balance due on or before the shoot day.

For corporate clients coordinating large team sessions or multi-day events, it's recommended to book at least three to four weeks in advance to ensure date availability.

For individual headshot sessions, one to two weeks is usually sufficient. For corporate events, team headshot days, or multi-person portrait sessions, booking three to six weeks ahead is strongly recommended — particularly for events with fixed dates like conferences or product launches.

Wear what represents how you want to be perceived professionally. A few practical tips:

  • Solid colors photograph better than busy patterns or logos
  • Bring two or three outfit options to give yourself variety
  • Avoid large, distracting jewelry unless it's part of your brand
  • For actors, bring options that reflect different character types
  • Clothes should be clean, pressed, and well-fitted

A detailed prep guide is sent after booking.

Deliverables & turnaround

Headshot and portrait sessions are typically delivered within five to seven business days. Corporate event galleries are generally delivered within seven to ten business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee — please mention your deadline at booking.

High-resolution JPEG files are delivered via a private online gallery, sized for both print and web use. LinkedIn-optimized and web-ready versions are included at no extra cost. If your agency, publication, or platform requires a specific format, size, or color profile, just let us know and files will be prepared accordingly.

Professional editing — including color correction, exposure balancing, and skin retouching — is included on all selected final images. The goal is a natural, polished result that looks like you on your best day, not an over-processed version. Additional retouching requests (composite work, background replacement, etc.) can be arranged for an additional fee.
